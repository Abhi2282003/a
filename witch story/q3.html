Moon's Orbit: The Moon orbits Earth in an elliptical path as well. This lunar orbit takes about 27.3 days to complete. The plane of the Moon's orbit is slightly inclined to Earth's orbit, which means that the Moon doesn't always appear in the same plane as the Sun and Earth.

Earth's Orbit: Earth orbits the Sun in an elliptical (oval-shaped) path. This orbit takes about 365 days to complete. The plane of Earth's orbit is known as the "ecliptic plane."
